System Requirements

  • Processor: 64-bit, 1 GHz or faster
  • RAM: 2 GB minimum, 4 GB recommended
  • Storage: 20 GB minimum, 40 GB recommended
  • Display: 1024x768 resolution minimum
  • Internet: Broadband connection for updates and downloads

Installation Guide

Follow our step-by-step guide to install BumbleOS on your system:

  1. Download the ISO file for your preferred version of BumbleOS.
  2. Create a bootable USB drive using tools like Rufus or Etcher.
  3. Insert the USB drive into your computer and restart it.
  4. Boot from the USB drive and follow the on-screen instructions to install BumbleOS.
  5. After installation, remove the USB drive and reboot your system.
